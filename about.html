PicCBuilder Eclipse Plug-in

The PicCbuilder Eclipse Plug-in is an Eclipse CDT Manged Build Extension for Microchip PIC Toolchains like C30, C32 and C18 (under development) and supports the development of managed C applications for PIC24H, PIC24F, PIC30, PIC33, PIC32 and PIC18 (under development) series of processors.
